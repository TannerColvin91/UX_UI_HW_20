About me!

A freelance UX/UI designer, creative, and lifelong sci-fi nerd with unique skills in cinematography, VFX, and motion graphics. I also hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Japan Studies from Montana State University and studied abroad at Kumamoto Gakuen University.

Through visual and UX design, I absolutely enjoy approaching user problems with a mindset of curiosity and empathy to create experiences that are impactful and long-lasting. My skills include Figma, DaVinci Resolve, Google Office, and Illustrator. My approach involves being an individual who does his best to put himself in other people’s shoes. My roles performed include conducting stakeholder interviews, storyboarding, graphic design, and creating components.
